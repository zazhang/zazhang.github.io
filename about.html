About Zhizun Zhang

I'm a fifth year doctoral student in the Chemical Engineering Department of Columbia University.

My reserach interests include quantitative modeling, data analysis, machine learning, risk management, and computer-aided knowledge management.

Before Columbia, I received my bachelor's degree from University of Kentucky. Besides research, I work on my own start-up.
